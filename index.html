The Haunting of Castle Moher

In the emerald hills of western Ireland, where cliffs meet crashing waves and mists curl through winding roads, a tragic and beautiful tale lingers in the walls of an ancient castle.

Princess Christina Leigh Rose—beloved artist and free spirit—rode her motorcycle under the twilight sky, the wind full of sea salt and freedom. But fate had other plans. A sharp bend, a sudden slip, and everything changed. Her body was never buried, her spirit never passed on. Instead, she became a whisper within Castle Moher, a ghost bound not by chains, but by longing.

Within the castle, time folds like parchment. Rooms shift. Shadows hum with memory. And deep within the stained-glass heart of the castle—the Sanctum of Light—her sleeping body rests beneath kaleidoscopic rays, waiting.

Word of Christina’s strange half-life drifted north through wind and song until it reached the ears of a noble soul: Prince Matthew Gordon Blaise of County Bray. Known for his courage, wit, and the poems he kept tucked into his coat, the prince set out at once, vowing to bring the princess back.

But restoring life is not so simple. To awaken Christina from her enchanted slumber, the prince must collect three sacred remnants of her soul…
